ABOUT

We are a team in Girls Who Code that designed a website to help break the period stigma. In our website we hope to inform girls about the changes happening in their bodies, specifically due to periods. We are girls and we understand the struggles with dealing with periods and the pain that we must sit through. In our website we hope to help people understand what periods are, how to deal with them, decreasing the pain of cramps and much more. We want to also bring awareness to girls in poverty who are not educated about periods and who canâ€™t afford materials to help deal with them such as pads and tampons. We hope our website can help in some way! It is the 21st century already. We have gone to the moon, we can talk to people thousands of miles away, and we can hold a computer in our hands. Periods shouldn't hold anyone back. It is time to free girls from the chains of period stigma.
