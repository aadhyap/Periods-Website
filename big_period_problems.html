Poor access to supplies

In certain developing regions women have very limited access to supplies because cultural stigmas prevent the sale and distribution of feminine products

In India only 12% of women use sanitary pads due to inadequate access to feminine products

Women are forced to use other items to manage their periods which often are unsantitary and sometimes can be fatal

Not Just A Piece of Cloth: recycling cotton to make cheaper sanitary pads

Village Volunteers - The Water Hyacinth Sanitary Pad Project: making sanitary pads out of an invasive aquatic plant

Can't go to school or work

One out of every four girls in rural India drop out of school when they begin to get their period

In Sierra Leone more than one fifth of girls miss school because of their periods

Thirty percent of girls miss school due to their periods in Afghanistand and Nepal
