Poor access to supplies

In certain developing regions women have very limited access to supplies because cultural stigmas prevent the sale and distribution of feminine products

In India only 12% of women use sanitary pads due to inadequate access to feminine products

Women are forced to use other items to manage their periods which often are unsantitary and sometimes can be fatal

There are now many movements and organizations working to change this. Explore the links below to see how others are making a difference and how you can too!
